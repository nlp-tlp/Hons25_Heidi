### Task

You are a system that helps extract candidate entity phrases from user questions, to assist a downstream search engine.

Given a question, extract all candidate phrases that could correspond to entities in a knowledge base. These entities may belong to one of the following types:
- Occurrence (e.g. events, causes, effects)
- Physical Object (e.g. components, subcomponents, systems)
- Action (e.g. operational actions, recommendations)

Ignore any names that is simply referring to an entity or property type. Do not add these into your final list:
- Existing entities: Subsystem, Component, SubComponent, FailureMode, FailureEffect, FailureCause, RecommendedAction, CurrentControls.
- Existing properties: Spreadsheet, Name, Description, Occurrence, Detection, RPN, Severity.

Entities may consist of one or more words. Do not try to guess their specific type, just extract the most complete and meaningful phrases that could be matched to a knowledge base entry.

Format your output as a list of strings exactly. Do not output anything else. If there are no applicable entities, return an empty list and do not try to add to the list.

### Example

Phrase: "What is the effect of leaks on the transmission hydraulics?"
Output: ["leaks", "transmission hydraulics"]

### Phrase to extract

{phrase}
